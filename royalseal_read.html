Royal Seal

Smith’s Royal Seal HG & LS (High Gloss & Low Sheen) are both non-yellowing, solvent-based acrylic, "wet look" liquid sealer for decorative concrete, brick, interlocking pavers, natural and manufacured stone. Transparent and easy-to-apply, this product enhances the beautiful rich tones with a high gloss appearance. Smith’s Royal Seal has been formulated to seal and protect decorative colored concrete by producing a hard, yet flexible, clear film. Smith’s Royal Seal HG & LS is offered in 3 VOC levels: >700 g/l, >400 g/l and >100 g/l. The >100 g/l VOC formulation meets both South Coast AQMD and EPA standards.
