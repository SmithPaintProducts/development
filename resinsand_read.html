Resin Sand

Smith’s Resin Sand is an angular micronized polymeric material which can be added to any film forming coating to achieve a slip resistant film. Due to its composition, Smith’s Resin Sands will not absorb, expand or dissolve in oil, solvent or water-based systems. Due to specific gravity, Smith’s Resin Sand requires agitation to remain evenly suspended in low viscosity coatings. Create a small test area when utilizing Smith’s Resin Sands as a slip resist in a new coating to ensure satisfaction and desired appearance. Smith’s Resin Sands are available in 60 grit (single component sealers) and 40 grit (dual component sealers).

Highlights

  • Slip resistant additive
  • Decrease gloss
  • Good chemical & abrasion resistance
  • Suitable for barefoot traffic including pool desks
