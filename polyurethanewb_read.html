Polyurethane WB

Smith’s Polyurethane WB is a dual component, film forming, water-based polyurethane applied as a protective or maintenance coat over Smith’s Color Floor, Color Wall and Color Accents. This 53% high solid, non-flammable, superior abrasion resistant water-based system offers comparable characteristics associated with traditional solvent-based polyurethanes while eliminating the hazardous volatility and environmental concerns. Smith’s Polyurethane WB conforms to Source Specific Standards Rule 1113 regarding VOC limits established by AQMD in California. This sealer was developed for interior or exterior extreme wear surfaces in residential and commercial installations.
