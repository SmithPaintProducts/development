Royal Tints

Smith’s Royal Tints to rejuvenate old slabs as well as fix undersired variances in color including, but not limited to integral colored slabs and color hardener installs. Smith’s Royal Tint has been successfully tested with numerous solvent-based sealers; test to make sure Smith’s Royal Tint is compatible with the solvent-based sealer of your choice.

WARNING: Only for use with solvent-based acrylic sealers!

Instructions

Smith’s Royal Tints are packaged in 1/2 galon containers, which will tint 5 gallons of Smith’s Royal Seal. Pour 2-3 gallons of Smith’s Royal Seal into a 5 gallon metal pail. Add contents of Smith’s Royal Tint container into 5 gallon pail, then add additional Royal Seal to achieve 5 gallons. Agitate with wooden stir stick or mechanically mix until uniform color is achieved. Apply product to a properly prepared substrate with a dip and roll method. The system was designed to achieve a monotone color with a 2 coat application on moderately profiled concrete, thus heavy textured concrete may require additional applications.
