Dry Pigment Packs

Smith’s Dry Pigment Packs are pre-measured to integrally color 50lbs (1 bag) of Smith’s 4 in 1 Overlay White (both fine and coarse). The colors represented on the Color Chart provide an approximation of the final color appearance when sealed with Smith’s Royal Seal (solvent-based acrylic sealer). Smith’s Dry Pigment Packs meet or exceed ASTM C-979 requirements.

 

Highlights

  • Achieves natural appearance
  • 16 colors 
  • Increased surface durability 
  • Ultra Violet Light Resistant
  • Non-blushing
  • Low VOC
  • Resistant to hot tire pickup
  • Interior and Exterior application
