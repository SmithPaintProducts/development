Super Release

Smith’s Super Release was developed as a clear parting agent between stamp tools and fresh concrete or polymer modified overlay products including Smith’s 4 in 1 Overlay. Smith’s Super Release should be sprayed over fresh concete, overlay or grout as well as to the texturing stamp creating a bond breaking barrier which prevents the tools from sticking or material pulling off. In addition, Smith’s Super Release prolongs tool life by decreasing friction between the stamping mats and the concrete or overlay. Although this liquid release has little to no residue on the treated area, It is recommended to clean the substrate with a neutral detergent for overlays or Smith’s Green Clean for concrete before the applicaion of sealers, Smith’s Color Floor, Color Wall or Color Accents.
